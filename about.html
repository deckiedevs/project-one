About the Staff!

Mila Decker

Mila is an Orlando-based web developer. After nine years of public service as a Crime Scene Investigator and Forensic Firearms Specialist, she decided it was time for a new challenge and enrolled in the University of Central Florida Full-stack Coding Boot Camp. Drawing on her problem-solving skills from her time as a forensic investigator, Mila quickly fostered a passion for coding. She is currently working hard towards completing the UCF Boot Camp and can't wait to start coding full time!

Jenny King

Jenny is a born and raised Floridian. She worked for 8 years with a background in accounting for a multi-millionaire in fields ranging from dermatology to property management. After the company was dissolved, she took her skills to Nashville and worked for an artist management company managing social media for the artist as well as running fan club events. After a move back to Florida and recent events, she decided to take a leap of faith and do a complete career change by attending the University of Central Florida Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp.

Ruben Matamoros

Ruben originally hail’s from the tropical land of Costa Rica, although, he grew up mostly here in the USA and now resides in Tampa Bay, Florida. He graduated at the top of his class in 2013 from Full Sail University as the Advanced Achiever, with a BA in Graphic Design, and since then has designed on a global scale with companies such as Ashley Furniture and Raymond James Financial, and also as a freelancer. After 7 years of designing, he decided it was time for a new challenge which will supplement design, and enrolled in the University of Central Florida’s Full-stack Coding Boot Camp. He is now passionate about completing the Boot Camp and diving into this new and exciting career!

Jorge Navarro

Jorge is a multi-skilled Data Analyst and Web Developer with a strong knowledge of HTML, CSS, Javascript, Jquery, Mongo DB, SQL, full-stack JavaScript development, Python applications, and data visualization development. A strong business development professional and graduate of Full Stack Web Development, Data Analytics and Visualizations programs at The University of Central Florida.
