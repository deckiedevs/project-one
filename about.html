About the Staff!

Mila Decker

Mila Decker is an Orlando-based web developer. After nine years of public service as a Crime Scene Investigator and Forensic Firearms Specialist, she decided it was time for a new challenge and enrolled in the University of Central Florida Full-stack Coding Boot Camp. Drawing on her problem-solving skills from her time as a forensic investigator, Mila quickly fostered a passion for coding. She is currently working hard towards completing the UCF Boot Camp and can't wait to start coding full time!
